⚙️ General

How Photopea opens and how images are placed on the canvas.

Auto follows the browser language. Pick a language to override.

DPI

Keyboard shortcut: Alt+Shift+P opens the last right‑clicked image in Photopea (customize in about:addons → gear → Manage Extension Shortcuts).

Permission “Access your data for all websites” is used only to download images that block hotlinking (CORS), so Photopea can open them.
